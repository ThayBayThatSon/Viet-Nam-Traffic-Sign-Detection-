1. Problem Statement

Why this topic?

  • Complex traffic conditions where drivers easily miss signs, leading to violations.
  • High demand for Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) and Autonomous Vehicles.
  • Minimizing traffic accidents through early warnings.

Current Challenges

  • Traffic signs in Vietnam are diverse with various shapes.
  • Often obscured by trees or affected by poor lighting/weather.
  • Requires an extremely fast recognition algorithm (Real-time).

3. Technology & Algorithm

YOLOv8n Model (Nano Version)

  • A State-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) architecture for Object Detection.
  • Major Advantage: Extremely fast recognition by scanning the image only once.
  • Version Choice: We chose YOLOv8n (Nano) as it is perfectly suited for deployment on low-power real-time edge devices (e.g., Dashcams).
  • Tools used: Python, PyTorch, Ultralytics.

3.1 Why we chose YOLOv8

Key Advantages

  • Super Fast (Real-time): It processes video frames instantly, which is crucial for dashcams and self-driving cars to warn drivers on time.
  • Highly Accurate: It is exceptionally good at finding small, blurry, or far-away traffic signs in complex environments.
  • Lightweight: It doesn't require a supercomputer. It can run directly on simple devices inside a car.

How does it work? (Simply put)

  • You Only Look Once: Instead of scanning the image piece by piece, the AI looks at the whole image at once, drastically reducing processing time.
  • Learning by Seeing: We provided it with over 3,000 photos of Vietnamese streets. The AI learned the unique shapes and colors of our signs to detect them automatically.

8. Limitations & Future Work

Limitations

  • Dataset Class Imbalance: Some signs have 1000+ images while others have < 10, reducing accuracy for rare signs.
  • Photos taken at night, in severe low-light conditions, or with intense headlight glare can reduce accuracy.
  • Physically deformed signs or signs obscured by trees more than 80%.

Future Work

  • Address data imbalance by collecting more data for rare classes (Data Augmentation).
  • Deploy directly onto embedded Dashcams (e.g., Raspberry Pi/Jetson Nano).
  • Integrate a Voice Alert system for drivers.
